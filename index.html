Abstract

FashionDiffusion explores the application of diffusion models to the generation of fashion imagery. We investigate conditional image synthesis for apparel design, focusing on texture fidelity, structural coherence, and controllable style variation. Our approach demonstrates that diffusion models can produce high-quality fashion samples suitable for downstream design workflows.

Method

Overview of the FashionDiffusion pipeline. A conditional diffusion model is trained on fashion images to generate high-fidelity apparel designs.

Results

Generated samples demonstrating diversity across clothing categories.

Conclusion

Our results show that diffusion models are a promising tool for fashion image synthesis. Future work includes higher resolution training and interactive conditioning for real-world design applications.
